About Arjun

I am a recent graduate of the University of Chicago where I majored in math and computer science and completed a Master’s degree in computer science.

At UChicago, I worked with Professor Andrew A. Chien and the Large Scale Systems Group on hardware acceleration and the benefits of better data representation.

Currently, I’m working on building PandaSQL, a better platform for scalable data science with Rohan Kumar and Professor Raul Castro Fernandez.
